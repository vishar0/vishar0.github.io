---
title: AI's biggest impact will be in education
date: 2026-05-10
published: true
---

The best thing about LLMs is that they have made learning _so much_ more accessible and fun. Everyone now has an individualized tutor that can, and is willing to, meet them at their level.

This is remarkable for countries like India where appetite far outstrips access. I benefited from the equalizing effects the Internet brought about growing up, and saw them all around me. This will be bigger.

There was a [tweet](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2049907410303865030) making the rounds recently about how you can now outsource your thinking, but you cannot outsource your understanding. And understanding is now there for the taking.
