---
title: A definition of continual learning
date: 2026-04-18
---

Continual learning is widely talked about these days. As far as I can tell, there is no consensus on a single definition. The clearest articulation I've come across is Section 3 of [this 2021 benchmark paper on continual RL](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.10919).

It's a good definition to stress test in-context learning against.
